FAQ

Questions answered.

Do I need to enter any API keys or configure anything?
No. VAYU OS manages all AI infrastructure centrally. You connect Gmail (1 click), scan a WhatsApp QR code, and upload your CSV. That's the entire setup.
How does the WhatsApp connection work?
Click "Connect WhatsApp" and a QR code appears. Scan it with your phone's WhatsApp app. The session is stored securely server-side — no WhatsApp Business API or paid account needed.
What happens after my 7-day trial ends?
All AI agents pause automatically. Your data stays completely intact — leads, conversations, meeting history. Upgrade to continue. No data loss, ever.
How is VAYU OS different from Apollo, Instantly, or other outreach tools?
Those are tools. VAYU OS is an operating system. It handles email, WhatsApp, voice, inbox, and AI insights — in one unified system, without you touching any settings. It's not a sequence builder, it's an autonomous revenue engine.
Is my data secure? Where is it stored?
All data is stored on encrypted Neon PostgreSQL (EU/US region). Gmail tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Webhook events use HMAC-SHA256 signature verification. Your data is never shared or sold.
